Brief Introduction:

V Guard Portal (“Portal”) is being developed by few Logistic Industry players committed with an intent to avoid infusion of unethical employees having bad malicious track record as per the records available with the employer updating the Portal

 

Objective:

Portal provides an easy platform for the updation and/ or maintenance of database of the unethical employees more particularly defined hereunder the Terms and Conditions of this Portal

 

Scope:

The data to be updated on the Portal covers all types of employees such as permanent employees, employees on deputation, on secondment, probationers, trainees, apprentices, contractual/ outsourced employees or ad hoc or daily wage workmen etc. who are found to be of unethical track record by the concerned employers

 

Motto:

To guard against the employment of unscrupulous/ unethical employees having unethical track record with the employer

 

Terms and Conditions:

  1. The details of only Unethical Employees are to be updated on the Portal

  2. The “Unethical Employees” for the purpose of this Portal shall mean those who are found to be of unethical conduct or involved in any malicious or fraudulent activities including but not limited to conspiracy, criminal breach of trust, impersonation at the expense of the company, misappropriation of company’s property or cash, or any other activity/ involvement justifying malafides/ criminal action attributable on the part of the employee or found to be engaged in permeating negativity amongst the employees of the company with an intent to defame the company or any other activity justifying the furtherance of objectives detrimental to the interest of the company or having divulged or found to be divulging of confidential information of the company and/ or indulged in unauthorised solicitation of the clients/ associates of the company for gaining personal benefit in violation of the employment terms. The said unethical conduct also comprise grave or gross behavioural aspects indicating unwarranted provocations either to other employees or to clients/customers/partners/associates of the company including enticing sexually coloured/ illicit remarks/ advances in any manner or form, while on duty

  3. That it is being acknowledged by the concerned employer updating the Portal that no such employee is being updated on the Portal who in the opinion of the concerned employer is being terminated on account of dissatisfactory performance or is unable to meet any particular performance criteria set up by the company or against whom any full and final settlement dues are pending to be recovered by the company unless the said employee has left the company without any intimation being served to the company and without settling the said dues with the company despite a prior written notification to settle the pending dues being served by the company to the concerned employee;

  4. The it is being acknowledged that the database on the Portal is purely for reference purposes only;

  5. That it is being acknowledged by the employer updating the Portal that such updation is exclusively based on the true, correct, verifiable and justifiable records available with the said employer;

  6. The names appearing in the database is not to be considered as the final and/ or conclusive certification or opinion pertaining to the conduct/ character of the concerned employee in any manner whatsoever;

  7. That the said updation is required to be supplemented with independent verification to be conducted by anyone acting upon the database maintained on the Portal;

  8. That it is being acknowledged by the employer updating the Portal that the said updation is not based on any personal biasedness or prejudices, likes and dislikes for a person;

  9. That it is being acknowledged by the employer updating the Portal that in no circumstance the said updation is with an intent to denigrate or defame the image of the employee updated on the Portal;

  10. That it is being acknowledged by anyone accessing the Portal that the reflection of the name of the employee hereunder this Portal is not to be used as any evidential record justifying the malafides/ character of the said employee in any litigation/ court cases whatsoever;

  11. That anyone accessing/ agreeing to act based on the information available on the Portal has thoroughly read the contents stipulated hereunder and has acted thereupon based upon its own investigation and in no circumstance the administrators/ operators of the Portal are liable to be held responsible in any manner whatsoever

  12. That where consequent upon updation on the Portal, the employer subsequently observes/discovers that new facts have come into picture which justifies the bona fides of the updated employee then in such an eventuality, the said employer acknowledges to stipulate the bonafides of the updated employee vide insertion of a suitable remark justifying the same at the remarks column available on the Portal


 

Disclaimer:

That it is being warranted and represented by those accessing the Portal or acting upon the information available on the Portal or providing any updations hereunder this Portal that the administrators/ operators of the Portal are just facilitators/ consolidators/ aggregators of the list concerning the database of unethical employees based upon the records available with the concerned employers exclusively and in no circumstance shall the said administrators/ operators of the Portal be held to be liable or responsible for any false or untrue information or claims by any person as to the names appearing in said database particularly when the Portal is being created with an intent to avoid infusion of the unethical employees based on the records available with the concerned employers exclusively and not to vilify the image of any person. Also when the said updation is in furtherance of the beneficial cause to avoid placement of unethical employees in the industry. It is being thoroughly acknowledged by the users of the Portal that the administrators/ or operators are to be held harmless incase of any unwarranted claims or disputes raised by anyone, as to the information/ database maintained on Portal

 

Suggestions:

Created with and motivated by the beneficial cause to avoid placement of Unethical Employees and benefit the logistic industry players, more particularly the society at large, the companies falling under the said logistic umbrella are encouraged to update the list of said Unethical Employees based on honest revelations as per the records available with them and further make consistent efforts to populate the database and propagate the said effort so that maximum benefit can be derived by the implementation of the said beneficial cause hereunder this Portal

Further the best employers practice demands that while updation at the Portal, the concerned employers understand a clear cut differentiation between non performance and unethical conduct as categorised hereunder this Portal since non performance is very subjective/ contextual in nature and any such updation based on the said criteria can have an adverse impact on the career of the concerned employee

Also under the appointment letters to be issued to the employees, the employers may reserve a right to themselves for updation of name of employee on Portal incase where employee is found to be unethical or leaves the company without complying with post termination obligations thereby promoting ethical conduct amongst employees
